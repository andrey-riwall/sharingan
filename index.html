Sharingan

an advanced form of the Sharingan that only a few members of the Uchiha clan have been able to awaken. The Mangekyo Sharingan has been described as "the heavenly eyes that unimpededly see the truth of all creation" (天 壌 の 理 を 掌握 せ し 瞳, tenjo no kotovari about shoakusehi hitomi).

Mangekyō Sharingan

Kekkei Genkai and Dojutsu of the Uchiha clan, which is selectively manifested among its representatives. It is revered as one of the Sandai Dojutsu (三大 瞳 術, "The Three Great Eye Techniques"), along with the Byakugan and Rinnegan. Although the Sharingans abilities originated from the Rinne Sharingan of Otsutsuki Kaguya, it was first awakened as a separate dojutsu by Otsutsuki Indra (Otsutsuki Hagoromo in the anime).
